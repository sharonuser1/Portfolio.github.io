Experience

I’ve been fortunate to gain hands-on experience both academically and in industry. Below are some key highlights of my journey:

👩‍💻 CoderDojo Volunteer — Maynooth University

My first technical experience began at university, where I volunteered with CoderDojo. I collaborated with other demonstrators to guide children aged 8–16 through coding exercises using Scratch and Microbit. It was a highly engaging, hands-on experience that helped develop my mentoring and communication skills.

🧑‍🏫 Computer Science Center (CSC) Tutor — Maynooth University

In my second academic role, I worked at the CSC where I tutored first-year students. I assisted with weekly assignments, coding projects, and provided one-on-one help with various programming challenges. This experience improved my problem-solving and peer support skills.

🏢 Unum Internship (Taster Program) — Industry Experience

I was selected for a two-week internship with Unum, a U.S.-based insurance company with its tech operations in Ireland. I shadowed professionals across different roles, attended team meetings, Q&As with former & current interns, and had 1-on-1s with Delivery Managers, UX Researchers, Business Analysts, Developers, and Software Engineers. This gave me first-hand insights into the corporate tech world.
